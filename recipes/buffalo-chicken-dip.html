Buffalo Chicken Dip

Bake chicken

Preheat oven to 400ºF. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil. Coat chicken with olive oil and S&P. Bake for 15-20mins (or until cooked through). Remove and set aside to cool. Lower oven temperature to 350ºF.

Sautée onions & garlic

Dice onion (1/2 medium onion) and mince garlic (3 cloves). Heat ghee (2 tbsp) small skillet over medium heat. Sautée onion until soft. Add garlic and cook until softened. Remove from heat.

Make dip

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the mayo (2/3 cup), coconut cream (2/3 cup), mustard (1 Tbsp), garlic powder (1 tsp), onion powder (1 tsp), dill (1 tsp), paprika (1/2 tsp), hot sauce (1/2 cup), and lemon juice (1- 1/2 Tbsp) until smooth. Shred chicken breasts. Mix iin chicken and cooked garlic & onions to dip.

Bake

Transfer dip to small casserole dish. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 mins (or until outer edges bubble and dip is heated through).
