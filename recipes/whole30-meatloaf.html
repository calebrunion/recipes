Prepare meatloaf

Cook onions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 ºF
  2. In a medium skillet, heat ghee (2 Tbsp) on medium
  3. Sautée onions (1/2 medium onion) until soft
  4. Add garlic (3 cloves) and a pinch of salt
  5. Cook for another minute or so
  6. Remove from heat

Prepare meat

  1. In a large bowl, combine beef (2 lbs), eggs (2 eggs), almond flour (1/3 cup), ketchup (3 Tbsp), cooked onion/garlic, salt (1 tsp), mustard (2 tsp), italian seasoning (1 tsp), and black pepper (1 pinch).
  2. Mix with hands - don't over do it
  3. Transfer to a loaf pan (9x5)
  4. Bake for 20 mins
  5. Remove from oven
  6. Spread ketchup (1/3 cup) on top
  7. Bake another 40-45mins (or until cooked through: 160 ºF
  8. Let cool for 10 mins - serve garnished with parsley
