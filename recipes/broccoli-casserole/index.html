Broccoli Casserole

Roast!

  1. Preheat oven to 400 ºF
  2. Shake the following in a plastic bag:
    • chicken (1 lb)
    • olive oil (2 Tbsp)
    • salt (1 pinch)
  3. Place on a baking sheet
  4. Shake the following in a plastic bag:
    • broccoli (1 lb)
    • olive oil (2 Tbsp)
    • salt (1 pinch)
  5. Place on another baking sheet
  6. Place both chicken and broccoli in oven
  7. Roast chicken for 30 minutes (flip midway through)
  8. Roast broccoli for 20 mins

Sauce!

  1. Cook bacon (½ lb) on medium in large sauce pan until crisp
  2. Remove bacon
  3. Add onions (1 onion, chopped) and cook until soft
  4. Add garlic (4 cloves, minced) and cook until soft
  5. Whisk the following into the pan:
    • broth (1 ¾ cups)
    • tapioca flour (2 Tbsp)
    • coconut milk (1 ½ cups)
    • mustard (1 Tbsp)
    • nutritional yeast (2 Tbsp)
  6. Raise temperature and bring to a boil while stirring
  7. At boil, lower heat to simmer for another minute until thick
  8. Stir in the following:
    • salt & pepper (to taste)
    • sage (1 tsp, minced)
    • rosemary (1 Tbsp, minced)
  9. Stir in cauliflower rice (12 oz) until soft
  10. Remove from heat

Bake!

  1. Cut chicken into bitesize pieces
  2. Add chicken, broccoli, and mixture to cassarole dish
  3. Crumble bacon and sprinkle all over
  4. Bake for 20 minutes

Enjoy!
