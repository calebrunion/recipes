Broccoli Casserole

Cook

  1. Add rice (½ cup) & water (1 cup) to a pot on high
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat to simmer until water is gone

Roast!

  1. Preheat oven to 400 ºF
  2. Shake the following in a container:
    • broccoli (2 heads)
    • olive oil (2 Tbsp)
    • salt (1 pinch)
  3. Place on a baking sheet
  4. Roast broccoli for 20 mins

Sauce!

  1. Heat cooking oil (2 Tbps) in a large sauce pan
  2. Add onions (1 onion, chopped) and cook until soft
  3. Add garlic (4 cloves, minced) and cook until soft
  4. Whisk the following into the pan:
    • broth (1½ cups)
    • almond flour (2 Tbsp)
    • coconut cream (1 can)
    • mustard (1 Tbsp)
  5. Raise temperature and bring to a boil while stirring
  6. At boil, lower heat to simmer until thick
  7. Stir in the following:
    • salt & pepper (to taste)
    • sage (1 tsp, minced)
    • rosemary (1 Tbsp, minced)
  8. Stir in cauliflower rice (12 oz) until soft
  9. Remove from heat

Bake!

  1. Cut chicken into bitesize pieces
  2. Add chicken, broccoli, and mixture to cassarole dish
  3. Crumble bacon and sprinkle all over
  4. Bake for 20 minutes

Enjoy!
